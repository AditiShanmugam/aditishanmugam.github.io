Multi-Modal Machine Learning for Object Detection.

This work was conducted as part of the course completion requirement for my Undergraduate degree under the guidance of Prof.Pratibha N , assistant professor at the Department of Electronics and Telecommunications Engineering at BMS Institute of Technology and Management. The main aim of this project is to demonstrate the significant improvements in Computer Vision tasks upon integrating contextual understanding used in Natural Language Processing tasks. The Contrastive Language Image Pretraining task uses multimodal data, Image-Text pairs, in Zero-shot or Few-shot settings. This work has been accepted to the 6th International Joint Conference on Advances in Computational Intelligence (IJCACI 2022).

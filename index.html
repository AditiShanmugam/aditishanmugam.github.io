Projects



Multi-Modal Machine Learning for Object Detection.

This work was conducted as part of the course completion requirement for my Undergraduate degree under the guidance of Prof.Pratibha N , assistant professor at the Department of Electronics and Telecommunications Engineering at BMS Institute of Technology and Management. The main aim of this project is to demonstrate the significant improvements in Computer Vision tasks upon integrating contextual understanding used in Natural Language Processing tasks. The Contrastive Language Image Pretraining task uses multimodal data, Image-Text pairs, in Zero-shot or Few-shot settings. This work has been accepted to the 6th International Joint Conference on Advances in Computational Intelligence (IJCACI 2022).

Source Free - Multi Label Domain Adaptation

This work introduces a novel concept of Source-Free Multi-Label Domain Adaptation (SF-MLDA) using graph convolution networks (GCN) and Co-teaching based methods. This is an ongoing research project which was a part of my internship with the Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore. My work was carried out in coalition with Vikash Kumar a Masters student and his advisor Prof.Anirban Chakraborty at the Indian Institute of Science, during my Internship at the Visual Computing Lab. The code for this work is currently underway and will be available upon publication of it's paper.

Superpixel Masking and Image Inpainting with Multi Exposure Fusion

Anomaly Detection and Correction using Supermixel Masking and Inpainting using a mask based curriculum learning and multi image exposure. Two variants of the network, GAN and Autoencoder based architectures were developed. I worked with Aditya Kumar Pal a former Masters student and his advisor Prof.Anirban Chakraborty at the Indian Institute of Science, during my Internship at the Visual Computing Lab. The code for the project is yet to be released.

Dry Waste Classification Using Machine Learning and IoT
